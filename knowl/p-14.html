Adding/Subtracting Fractions If two fractions have a denominator in common, add/subtract the numerators and place the result over the common denominator:
\begin{equation*} \frac{a}{c}+\frac{b}{c}=\frac{a+b}{c}\qquad\text{and}\qquad\frac{a}{c}-\frac{b}{c}=\frac{a-b}{c} \end{equation*}
If the fractions do not share a common denominator, rewrite the fractions so that they do.
